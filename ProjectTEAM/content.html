Fun Facts About Pokémon Lore

1. Mew is the ancestor of all Pokémon

Mew contains the genetic codes of every Pokémon, making it the ancestor of all known species.

2. Legendary Pokémon are tied to natural elements

For example, Groudon represents land, Kyogre represents sea, and Rayquaza rules the sky.

3. Pikachu was not always the mascot

Originally, Clefairy was considered as the Pokémon mascot before Pikachu became the face of the franchise.

4. Pokémon have origins in real-world myths

Many Pokémon are inspired by animals, folklore, or mythical creatures from different cultures.
